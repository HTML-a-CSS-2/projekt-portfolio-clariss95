About this Project

I made this website to share my plants and their lives. And also to motivate myself and others to learn more about keeping plants alive. I plan to keep track of the wellbeing of my plants here (so far it is rather poor...). And I am hoping to motivate every amateur plant care taker, such as myself, to do better and to not give up! Our plants NEED us!
